The 2020 presidential campaign of Pete Buttigieg, the mayor of South Bend, Indiana, began with the formation of an exploratory committee for the Democratic nomination on January 23, 2019. The campaign was formally launched on April 14, 2019. Buttigieg is the first openly gay Democratic candidate for president. If elected, Buttigieg would be the youngest (and the first millennial) and first openly gay U.S. president.Buttigieg supports single-payer healthcare, labor unions, universal background checks for guns, protecting the environment by addressing climate change, a pathway to citizenship, and passing a federal law banning discrimination against LGBT people.
